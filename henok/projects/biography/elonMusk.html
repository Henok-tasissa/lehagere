Biography of Elon Musk

Entrepreneur and the founder of Tesla Motors, SpaceX, and PayPal Elon Reeve Musk was born in June 28, 1971 in Pretoria, South Africa. As a young boy Musk was obsessed with reading different kinds of books and He was interested in computers and sci-fi. When he was young boy he used to get bullied in school. His classmates pushed him down a concrete stairwell and he was also beaten so badly that he needed to go to the hospital. Musk's father was emotionally abusive and tough he used to order Musk and his brother to sit silent for four hours as he lectured them. Later on, at the age of 17 Musk leave South Africa and moved to Canada.

In 1999 Musk co-founded X.com which later become PayPal and acquired by eBay for $1.5 million in stock. After that in 2002 Musk founded his third company, Space Exploration Technologies Corporation or SpaceX. On May 22, 2012 Space X launched its Falcon 9 rocket into space with an unmanned capsule. The vehicle was sent to the International Space Station with 1000 pounds of supplies for astronauts This was the first time a private company had sent a spacecraft to the international space station.

In March 2017, SpaceX successfully tested a flight and landing Falcon 9 rocket made from reusable parts, and it opened the door for more affordable space travel saving a lot of money. In February 2018 SpaceX launched the powerful Heavy rocket. The rocket was armed with additional Falcon 9 boosters and it can carry immense payloads into orbit. Falcon 9 heavy was given payload of Musk's cherry-red Tesla Roadster, equipped with cameras to "provide some epic views" for the vehicle's planned orbit around the sun.

Elon Musk is also the co-founder of Tesla Motors and product architect. The company produces affordable, mass-market electric cars as well as battery products and solar roofs. In 2008 after the formation of the company created a sport car capable of accelerating from 0 to 60 mph in 3.7 seconds. This car travels 250 miles between charges.

Image credit : https://cleantechnica.com

Elon Musk's inventions didn't end here, but he started working on many other projects. He is now working on Hyperloop which is a concept for a new form of transportation; Ai with a mission of advancing digital intelligence to benefit humanity. Other project he is working on is The Boring Company which is a way to reduce traffic by devoting resources to boring and building tunnels. Another project he is working on is High-speed train which is to build and operate a high-speed rail line.
