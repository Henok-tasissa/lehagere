Biography of Ada Lovelace

In the world dominated by men, there are only few women who stand up alone and be noticed in the years. Ada Lovelace was one of them and she was gifted women who knew a lot about computers. She was a great mathematicians and she helped computer to develop first instruction that was converted into computer program. During 1800s women do not know for such talents as many refer this kind of work as a man’s job. She was also known as first computer programmer and she encouraged women to explore their knowledge and skills and make it as their career. She understood mathematics in her early age and she understood lot of computer concepts. Her work still influence today’s world. She is also considered as mother of computer programming and the enchantress of numbers. She was given many names because of her genius ability.

Image credit : https://www.biography.com

During Lovelace’s discoveries women were beginning to take some education so the attitude of common people was that women can never match the skills and qualification of men. There were some rumors that the women who will study extensively would either become ill and eventually will die. Lovelace was driven and come in the eyes of everyone because of her passion and love for mathematics. She had incredible writing ability of computer programs. Ada mom recognizes her talent in the beginning and she hired one tutor for her to enhance her logical thinking. Her studies included science and mathematics. She wanted her daughter to get a chance to develop her career even though it was very uncommon those days. Ada worked on analytical engine which was a computer that time and she was able to translate language from the content to other languages to help other engineers to develop the program.
