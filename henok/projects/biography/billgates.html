Biography of Bill Gates

Bill Gates is an American business Entrepreneur and computer programmer who is the co-founder of Microsoft .Microsoft is the world's the largest personal computer software company. He was born in October 28, 1965 in Seattle in United States of America. He was born as the successful lawyer father and successful teacher mother. He has been ranked among world's richest people starting from 1987.Bill Gates was encouraged from young age to be competitive in technology field. Also he was bright, curious, developed an interest in computer while in school and wrote his first computer programs a young teenager. Bill Gates has grown up in Seattle with two sisters. His father William was an Attorney and his mother Mary was a school teacher. After completing his school, he was enrolled at Harvard College, however he did not stay here long enough to complete his studies.Later, and he dropped out to pursue his passion in computers and teamed up with Paul Pallen, his former schoolmate to form Microsoft.

In 1986, Bill Gates was 30 years old poses in front of boxes of Microsoft products. The packaging facility of the company's new 40-acre corporate campus in Redmond in Washington.Also, Microsoft held an initial public offering of 2.5 billion shares. By the end of the year, Gates had become a billionaire at the age of 31.Microsoft was the first company to dominate the personal computer market with its system and window platform.
